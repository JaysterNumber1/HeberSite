Heber City, Utah

The Home of the Train

Dairy Keen, home of not just trains but also several state awards, is a very popular choice for burgers, shakes, and even seasonal soups!

A Sleeper Pick?

Food Trucks are quite abundant in Heber, and they are great option to pick! One of them, Lola's, has become quite a popular place.

Very Varied Options

While this might seem a bit out of place, Ichiban is a great choice for an experience that is very different from your typical Utahn food joint.
